Importance of good posture

Working for hours in one position will do you no good.

Poor Posture can lead to pain

...in the neck, back, and shoulder regions. It can also lead to poor circulation.

Frequently stretching is beneficial

...for reduced chances of muscle injuries. Do it at least once every hour.

Proper posture is extremely important

...to keep your spine, bones and joints in the correct alignment.

Do you spend long hours studying or working while sitting?

Are online work environments or classes taking a toll on your physical health?

Then use our Posture Check website and Google Chrome extension for frequent reminders to exercise!

How our service benefits you

Here's how you can use our services to improve your work routine.

  • You feel overworked, stressed, and tired.

    Sitting at a desk (or worse, a bed or a couch) for long periods of time affects your posture, leading to back and neck pain.

  • You're smart though. You have installed our extension .

    The Posture Check extension allows you to input the frequency of reminders (example, remind every hour). It will remind you to correct your posture, take a break, and visit our website for more exercises!

  • You take a well deserved break from your notes.

    You take advantage of the useful exercises on our website or maybe go on a stroll.

  • Your posture improves and muscle pain reduces!

    Pretty irresistible, if you ask us.
